Valorant Agents and Roles

Within the game of Valorant, there are several different agents to choose from with a variety of roles they each play.

To shortly summarize, you play within a 5 man team where there can be duelists, initiators, controllers, or sentinels. Each character has a unique set of abilities to help the team secure a win.

Controller Omen Brimstone Controllers are adept to smoking off sections of the map and creating space for the team.
Duelist Reyna Raze Duelists are Entry Fraggers that seek out first engagements and remove enemies from play.
Initiator Sova Gecko Initiators help find info and initiate team entry on a map site.
Sentinel Sage Killjoy Sentinels help hold down sites for teamates, slowing down enemy entry and give teamates time to rotate over to help defend.

Since Omen is a controller, his abilities help him control parts of the map for his team as well as help him position himself to defeat enemies with ease.

Omen's Ablitities

Omen's has 3 key abilities along with an ultimate ability that help him control and take space on any map he plays on:

Smokes

  • Has 2 deployable smokes: One given for free, and the other bought pre round.
  • Can throw these smokes anywhere within ceratin range on ground, and on top of some surfaces.
  • Smokes linger for certain amount of time.
  • Timer begins after a smoke is thrown, once timer ends then another smoke is given.

Blind

  • Blind that can be thrown straight forward, blinds and deafens enemies and teammates alike.
  • Must be bought pre round.
  • Has limited width, but decent range going forwards.

Teleport

  • Has 2 short teleports, both must be bought pre-round.
  • Teleprts to target area seen within a certain range, can also teleport onto certain surfaces.
  • Shorter Range, faster time-to-teleport.

Ultimate Ability: From the Shadows

Finally, his ultimate ability, which is given after a certain number of 'charge orbs' have been filled, allows Omen to teleport to any area of the map, regardless of range.
